Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Comment Artist for Android ("Comment Artist" or the "App") handles information. The App was created by SungHoon Lee ("Developer", "we", "us", or "our").

Comment Artist is designed to work locally on your device. In the current version, the App does not require an account, does not show ads, and does not include analytics or crash-reporting SDKs.

Information the App Uses on Your Device

The App may access or use the following information only to provide its features:

  • Images you choose: If you select an image through Android's photo picker, the App accesses only the image you selected and converts it into braille-style text art on your device.
  • Text you enter and drawings you create: The App uses this content on your device to generate braille-style text art.
  • Saved outputs: If you save a generated result in the App's library, the App stores that result locally on your device. This may include the output name, source label, generated braille text, row and column size, format preset, favorite status, and creation time.
  • App settings: The App stores your language and theme preferences locally so they can be restored the next time you open the App.
  • Clipboard: When you tap a copy button, the App places the generated braille text on your device's system clipboard. After that, the clipboard is handled by Android and your device settings.

Information We Do Not Collect

In the current version, Comment Artist does not collect, upload, transmit, sell, or share your personal information with the Developer or third parties. In particular, the App does not collect or send:

  • your name, email address, phone number, or account information;
  • your precise or approximate location;
  • your contacts, messages, or call logs;
  • your advertising ID or other tracking identifiers;
  • usage analytics, crash reports, IP address, or device diagnostics to the Developer; or
  • your images, entered text, drawings, or generated outputs to a remote AI service or cloud processing service.

Third-Party Services and External Links

The current version of the App does not use third-party advertising, analytics, or crash-reporting services.

The App may contain links to external websites, such as open-source license pages. If you open an external link, that website is governed by its own privacy practices.

Data Retention and Deletion

Data stored by the App stays on your device until you remove it.

  • You can delete saved outputs from the App's library.
  • You can remove all locally stored App data by clearing the App's storage in Android settings or by uninstalling the App.
  • Content copied to the system clipboard remains there until it is overwritten, cleared, or otherwise removed by Android or your device settings.

Security

Saved outputs and settings are stored in the App's local app-private storage area provided by Android. No storage method is perfectly secure, but the App is designed so that your content stays on your device unless you choose to copy it to the system clipboard or open an external link.

Children's Privacy

Comment Artist is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children through the App.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date above.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or the App's privacy practices, contact the Developer at digitalspaceout.official@gmail.com.
